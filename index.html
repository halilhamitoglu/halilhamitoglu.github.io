About Glub

Glub is a mobile app for freshwater aquarium hobbyists. Manage multiple tanks, log water parameters, track fish and plants, schedule reminders, and learn through a built-in species encyclopedia.

🐠 Multi-tank inventory
💧 Water parameter trends
📅 Care reminders
📚 Species encyclopedia
🦠 Disease guide
🔬 Nitrogen cycle tracker
